About Us

SnAIke operates at the intersection of artificial intelligence and smart mobility, developing AI-driven solutions that enhance transportation efficiency, safety, and sustainability. Our business model revolves around providing AI-powered software and analytics to cities, transportation agencies, and mobility service providers, enabling them to optimize traffic flow, reduce congestion, and improve overall infrastructure performance and safety. At the core of our technology is a Digital Twin model, which creates virtual replicas of real-world transportation networks, allowing for real-time simulations, predictive analytics, and continuous optimization. By combining AI with smart city infrastructure, SnAIke aims to revolutionize urban transportation, making travel more seamless, connected, and environmentally responsible.
